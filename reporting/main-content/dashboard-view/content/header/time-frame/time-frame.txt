all_time_dropdown
Options:
Last 7 Days: Shows data from the past 7 days.
Last 30 Days: Shows data from the past 30 days.
Last 90 Days: Shows data from the past 90 days.
Last 180 Days: Shows data from the past 180 days.
Last 365 Days: Shows data from the past 365 days.
All Time: Displays all available data with no date restrictions.
Custom Range: Enables selection of a specific date range.

Default Behavior:
"All Time" is selected by default, displaying all report data based on the full available time frame. if any custom range is selected, the selected date range  report data will be shown on dropdown.
